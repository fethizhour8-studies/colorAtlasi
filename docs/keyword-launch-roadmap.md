# Keyword Launch Roadmap

This roadmap interprets the uploaded `keywords.xlsx` workbook for a fast, safe launch. It focuses on keywords with printable intent and avoids topics that depend on copyrighted characters, brands, celebrities, or recognizable franchise designs.

## What the workbook suggests

After deduplicating keyword rows, the strongest non-risky opportunities are:

| Priority | Cluster | Example keywords | Launch action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | General coloring | coloring pages, coloring sheets, free coloring pages, coloring page | Keep as top-level hub and homepage wording. Do not make one thin page for every generic variation. |
| 2 | Calendars | 2026 calendar, printable calendar, November calendar, October calendar, July calendar | Build evergreen calendar hub plus month pages. Avoid stale 2025 pages unless updating to current/future year. |
| 3 | Dinosaurs | ankylosaurus, brachiosaurus, dilophosaurus, parasaurolophus, therizinosaurus | Create one dinosaur collection plus individual dinosaur topic sections/pages as content grows. |
| 4 | Kids evergreen | coloring pages for kids, cute coloring pages, rainbow colors, train coloring page, fruit coloring pages | Add safe, broad kid-friendly collections first. |
| 5 | Mandalas | mandala, mandalas, mandala zum ausdrucken | Build adult/teen mandala pages with floral, geometric, simple, and detailed groups. |
| 6 | Animals | cat coloring pages, dog coloring pages, bear coloring pages, ocean animals coloring pages, farm coloring pages | Build animal collections with classroom-friendly copy. |
| 7 | Seasonal | christmas coloring pages, pumpkin carving ideas, Halloween generic terms | Build seasonal pages, but avoid copyrighted holiday characters. |
| 8 | Adult / relaxing | adult coloring pages | Add relaxing, cozy, floral, pattern, and mindful pages. |
| 9 | Activity printables | connect the dots printable, dot to dot printables, color by number printable | Good expansion after coloring pages and calendars. |

## Blocked by default

Do not target or generate content for:

- Disney, Pixar, Nickelodeon, Nintendo, Sanrio, Minecraft, Pokemon, Paw Patrol, Bluey, Sonic, Barbie, Marvel, DC, or similar franchise terms
- exact character names from movies, TV, games, anime, toys, or celebrity culture
- brand logos, mascots, costumes, or distinctive designs
- keywords where the search intent clearly expects a protected character

Competitors may rank with those topics, but this site should not copy that risk.

## Page depth target

For launch, each important collection should have:

- 300 to 800 useful words depending on page type
- 6 to 12 high-quality printables when possible
- what is included section
- best-for section
- how-to-print section
- related collections
- FAQ only when genuinely useful
- clear license note

## Competitor-inspired structure without copying

The workbook repeatedly shows competitor URLs using collection pages for broad topics, seasonal topics, calendars, dinosaurs, and animals. Use that structure as inspiration, not their wording or images:

- broad hub page
- topic collection page
- individual printable cards
- short download instructions
- usage ideas
- related collections
- strong internal linking

## Launch order

1. Keep homepage and top hubs focused on generic safe search terms.
2. Fill 5 to 8 strong collections before adding many thin topics.
3. Add dinosaur, animal, kids, mandala, adult, seasonal, and calendar printables first.
4. Add German/French/Spanish/Portuguese pages only after English pages are strong and translation workflow exists.
5. Review analytics weekly and expand what gets downloads, not only what has high volume.
